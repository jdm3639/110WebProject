Why Nosalgia?

I found this to be a very relevant and significant topic due to the sizable influx of nostalgic based media being incorporated into every form of the media industry to include film, game, and the music industry. I am going to be discussing the reasons nostalgic media is so effective, and more importantly, why this new wave of media has occurred and what societal and individual impacts are being caused by this new form of media.
